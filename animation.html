Who Is Mother Nature?

Style Frame

A styleframe is the first thing drawn for an aniamtion. It is sued to plan out the color pallete and help the aritst expiriment and define the style and colors they will use in the project. In a professional setting it is often the first thing that will be created in preporation for the animation.

Photoshop Animation

"Who is Mother Nature?"" is all about exploring the absract concept of who mother nature is in our everyday lifes as humans interact with thier expirenments. How she provides for earth and humans, as well as how we treat her back and the implications it has on earth.


Skiing Rotoscope

Style Frame

a rotoscope aniamtion is where you are taking footage either found or shot by the aritst that helps imply your making process for creating the aniamtion. Meaning you are tracing over or manipulating / adding to the video to combine both 4d and 2d media.

Rotoscope Animation

For this animation I complied footage I had taken of my brother skiiing in past years and edited those clips together to have a base for my animation. I then used photoshop and an aniamtion plug in to draw each and every frame seen in the vide. It took a very long time, but I am very happy with the result.


One Right Now - After Affects

After Affects Animation

While theres is not style frame for this animation, there was still alot of planning to go into it. This animation was made in after affects and animated to the song "One Right Now" by Post Malone and The WKND. By choosing a song, and clip of the song, and animating to the beat by manipualting shapes rather than drawing each frame, I did lots of research about the different affects I coudl create within this software. I then impleted what I learned into differnet parts of the video to match the music.
